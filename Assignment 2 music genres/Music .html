Popular Music Genres

  • Pop: Catchy, mainstream hits with mass appeal.
  • Rock: Electric guitars, drums, and powerful vocals.
  • Hip-Hop: Rhythmic speech (rapping) and beat-driven music.
  • Jazz: Improvisational style with brass and piano.
  • Classical: Rich orchestral arrangements from great composers.
  • Reggae: Rhythmic Caribbean sound with socially conscious lyrics.
